Drivy - Android

Drivy is the first peer-to-peer car sharing web and mobile app in France. Car owners can cover the cost generated by the car thanks to the rental of them personal car, and drivers can rent a car at a low price. The service includes an insurance that covers cars during rental.

The application embeds the following features:

  • For drivers:
    • Find the best car at the corner! (Location).
    • Contact the owner in one click (by phone or email), get agreement and book the owner car.
    • Receive real-time notifications.
    • Ask for rental extension with one click.
  • For owners:
    • Register your car for free takes in less than a minute.
    • Manage your rentals: accept, decline, or contact the driver.
